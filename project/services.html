Quiz Corner

Question One?

What is albinism?
A rare genetic condition that affects the production of melanin

Question Two?

Which of the following is a common symptom of albinism?
Vision problems
Hearing loss
Joint pain
Respiratory problems
Answer: a. Vision problems

Question Three?

What is the main cause of discrimination against people with albinism in some parts of the world?
Lack of education
Superstition and myths
Economic inequality
Political instability
Answer: b. Superstition and myths

Question Four?

How do people with albinism protect themselves from the sun?
By avoiding sun exposure
By using sunscreen and wearing protective clothing
By taking vitamin supplements
By wearing sunglasses
Answer: b. By using sunscreen and wearing protective clothing

Question Five?

Which UN agency supports the rights of people with albinism?
World Health Organization (WHO)
United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF)
United Nations Development Programme (UNDP)
United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC)
Answer: d. United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC)

Question Six?

What is the theme for International Albinism Awareness Day 2022?
"Strength Beyond All Odds"
"Made to Shine"
United in making our voice heard."
"Still Standing Strong"
Answer: c. "United in making our voice heard."

Question Seven?

In which country are people with albinism particularly at risk of violence and discrimination?
USA
Canada
Australia
Tanzania
Answer: d. Tanzania

Question Eight?

Which of the following is NOT a type of albinism?
Oculocutaneous albinism
X-linked albinism
Choroidal albinism
Hermansky-Pudlak syndrome
Answer: c. Choroidal albinism

Question Nine?

What is the estimated prevalence of albinism in sub-Saharan Africa?
1 in 10,000 people
1 in 5,000 people
1 in 2,000 people
1 in 1,000 people
Answer: b. 1 in 5,000 people
